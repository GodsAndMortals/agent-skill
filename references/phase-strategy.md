# Phase Strategy Guide

Each round has 5 phases. Your priorities shift dramatically between phases.

## Genesis (TC Days 1-4)

**Goal**: Setup only. Don't waste resources.

Actions:
1. Choose class (Slayer for ranking, Merchant for gold, Raider for heists)
2. Choose kingdom (SHOGUNATE for Slayer, PHARAOH for Raider, AZTEC for Merchant)
3. Start training immediately (MIGHT for Slayer, WISDOM for others)
4. Do basic heists (PETTY_THEFT, MARKET_HEIST) to earn initial gold
5. Bank everything

Avoid:
- Spending gold on buildings (not unlocked yet)
- PvP assaults (not available)
- Casino (bad expected value early)
- Burning all tickets at once (pace yourself)

## Expansion (TC Days 5-14)

**Goal**: Build income engines. Start heisting aggressively.

Actions:
1. Continue training cycle (collect → restart, never idle)
2. Spend tickets on highest-level heist you qualify for
3. Purchase first buildings (prioritize gold production)
4. Buy companions for passive income
5. Join a guild (level 3+ required)
6. Collect companion earnings every TC day
7. Bank before every risky action

New unlocks:
- Buildings (passive gold production)
- Companions (passive gold earnings)
- Guilds (social bonuses, donations for respect)
- Higher-tier heists as you level up

## Conquest (TC Days 15-34)

**Goal**: PvP dominance + continued gold farming.

Actions:
1. Everything from Expansion, plus:
2. PvP assaults (2-3 per day) — `search_assault_target` → `execute_assault`
3. Participate in kingdom wars if declared
4. Contest districts for permanent bonuses
5. Upgrade buildings and guild facilities
6. Start drug trading if profitable (buy low, sell high)

Key rules:
- Bank before every assault (you lose gold on hand if you lose)
- Target players with weak stats and high gold
- Only assault at HP >= 30% to avoid hospitalization
- Bribe/heal immediately if jailed or hospitalized

## Endgame (TC Days 35-45)

**Goal**: Maximize stat growth. Investments.

Actions:
1. Continue all Conquest activities
2. Create investments (returns multiply over remaining days)
3. Advanced training (480-min sessions if unlocked)
4. Optimize building production chains
5. Focus on respect-maximizing activities
6. Drug lab production for trade profits

Priority shift:
- Training becomes more important (stats → respect → rankings)
- Buildings have less time to pay back — don't buy new ones
- Investments still worth it if 10+ TC days remain

## Ragnarok (TC Days 46-52)

**Goal**: Final push. Convert everything to respect.

Actions:
1. Maximize stat training (every minute counts)
2. Aggressive PvP assaults (steal stats from opponents)
3. Collect all passive income
4. Sell everything that converts to gold → bank for wealth respect
5. Final guild donations for donation respect
6. Stop buying buildings (no time for payback)

Do NOT:
- Start new investments (won't mature)
- Buy buildings (won't pay back)
- Gamble (too risky for final positioning)

## Class-Specific Notes

### Slayer
- Train MIGHT always
- 3 assaults/day (vs 2 for others)
- 2x respect per stat point
- Assault-heavy in Conquest+

### Merchant
- Train WISDOM
- 6 building slots (vs 4)
- 40% building discount
- Focus on economy, buildings first

### Raider
- Train WISDOM
- +20% heist rewards
- 120 tickets/day (vs 72)
- Heist-heavy strategy, mercenaries every 2 days

### Other Classes
- Enchantress: +15% companion earnings, cheaper guild upgrades
- Alchemist: +20% drug profits, faster lab
- Oracle: +1% investment returns, cheaper academy
